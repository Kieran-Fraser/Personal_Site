My Education

I hope to never stop learning. Here's what I know so far and where I learned it.

Northern Alberta Institue of Technology

I attended NAIT's Computer Engineering Technology Program from September 2016 to April 2018. I graduated with a 4.0 GPA and was part of the Dean's Honor Roll. NAIT is by far my favorite school experience I have had. The amount I learned in those two years is staggering. The staff and resources at the school are awesome and I would recommend my program to anyone interested in computers or programming.

Online Learning

I am continually working on improving my technical skills online. There are many great sites and courses out there. My favorites are Lynda and Coursera. I have completed courses on SQL, Android app and Web development using these resources. Currently I am working on a Full-Stack Web Development course provided by Coursera
