Phone Controlled Car/Imporve Capstone Project

For my Capstone Project at NAIT. My partner and I built and designed a car that drives itself through a maze. This project involved using a decision-tree and node-based system for the car to navigate through the maze. We developed a software and hardware demonstration of this. We did have some consistency issues with our hardware model, due to the cheap parts we used. I intend to improve on the software part of the maze navigation and make it a simple AI that uses many iterations to determine the best path for the car.
I would also like to adapt the physical model of the car to work with a phone. This phone application will have a simple directional UI that will use Bluetooth to communicate with the model car. The car already has an Arduino Uno on it and a Bluetooth module that reads in data. This will be a fun extension of my project. I intent to scare my brother and friends with it.
