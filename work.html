Work Experience

I have worked a variety of entry-level and supervisory roles. Customer-service, Labour, Data Entry and Training are the areas I have the most experience in.

Connect Logistics

Connect Logistics is a liquor disturbing warehouse based in St. Albert AB. I worked here for around 3 years in several roles. Initially I started as a Warehouse Team Member and was on the floor picking liquor orders. This is a fast-paced role as there is a quota of around 1000 boxes a day to meet. I consistently met quota during and after my probation period. Because of this I was trained of some the more advanced machines like the reach truck and was moved into replenishment. After about a year of working on the floor, I was promoted to a Trainer. This is a safety focused supervisory role. I was responsible for teaching new hires how to reach quota and drive the machines safely. I would also perform safety operations on all warehouse team members to ensure regulations were being followed. Later I moved into the inventory department as a VAS Inventory Clerk. This was a data-entry and supervisory role. I was responsible for creating and prioritizing work orders of customer requests. Then I would delegate the orders to a small team and complete some myself.

Fionn MacCool's

Fionn MacCool's is a great Irish Pub that I still work at today. I bartend and serve at this establishment. The duties include cleaning and organizing the bar and pub, Serving and helping guests, as well as following all the Alberta Liquor and Gaming regulations. I started working here part-time while I was still at Connect Logistics, and have stuck with it for 2 years now. My Fionn's is located in the west end of Edmonton. Come by sometime!

Skip The Dishes

I also work part-time as food courier for Skip the Dishes. This is a flexible job that fit well with my schooling at NAIT. I also enjoy driving so I continue to deliver for Skip. I like the autonomy of setting my own hours and picking up the shifts I enjoy. The job is a customer-service based one as well. Drive to the restaurant to pick up the food and drop it off to the customer.
